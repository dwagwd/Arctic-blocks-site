How To Get Help

Use this page as the App Store Connect Support URL for Arctic Blocks. For the public site source and release-related repo details, use the repository link above. If you are testing a pre-release build, contact the person who provided the build.

What To Include

  • Device model and iOS version.
  • App version.
  • What you expected to happen and what happened instead.
  • Screenshots or logs, if available.

Game Center

For Game Center account, sign-in, or parental control issues, contact Apple Support in addition to using the support channel above.
