Arctic Blocks Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Arctic Blocks is a mobile puzzle game. This policy explains what information is processed when you use the app.

Information We Process

  • Gameplay progress stored locally on your device, including scores, challenge progress, and settings.
  • Advertising consent state, including whether you chose personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Ad request and delivery data processed by Google AdMob when ads are shown.
  • Game Center identifiers, leaderboard submissions, and achievement progress when you use Game Center features.

How Information Is Used

  • To save and restore your game progress and preferences.
  • To deliver ads and cap ad frequency.
  • To submit and display Game Center leaderboard and achievement data.
  • To improve stability and gameplay quality.

Third-Party Services

The app uses third-party services that may process data under their own privacy terms, including Apple Game Center and Google AdMob.

Data Retention

Locally stored game data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app data.

Children's Privacy

Arctic Blocks is not directed to children under 13. If you believe a child provided personal information, use the support page linked here and we will work to resolve it.

Contact

For privacy questions or requests, use the support page: Support.
